Research Journey

2022

Started research on bias in visual understanding pipelines

Worked with Prof. Olga Russakovsky at the Princeton Visual AI Lab on analyzing bias in skin lesion classification and reducing object hallucination in VQA models.

2023

Explored NLP and human-in-the-loop ML

Conducted research on LLM self-refinement for lie detection in Diplomacy games under Prof. Karthik Narasimhan.

2024

Senior thesis on explainable 3D perception

Developed a novel approach to reformulate tracking as inverse neural rendering under Prof. Felix Heide, receiving the Outstanding Computer Science Senior Thesis Prize.

Present

Research assistant at Princeton Computational Imaging Lab

Extending my senior thesis work on inverse generation for explainable 3D perception.
