# Monocular Human Reconstruction **Module 9.3** | [← Back to Index](../index.md.html) | [Previous: Neural Humans ←](09-humans-neural.md.html) --- ## Overview Monocular human reconstruction: Reconstruct 3D human shape and pose from single images or videos. Uses SMPL models and neural rendering for realistic results. --- ## Essential Papers ### HMR (2018) **End-to-End Recovery of Human Shape and Pose** (Kanazawa et al.) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1712.06584) **Key idea**: End-to-end learning to predict SMPL parameters from image. **Architecture**: CNN → SMPL parameters. **Training**: Weakly supervised with 2D keypoints, adversarial loss for shape. --- ### SPIN (2019) **SPIN: Learning to Reconstruct Humans in 3D from Sparse Views** (Kolotouros et al.) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1909.02533) **Key innovation**: Self-improving framework with optimization. **Process**: Predict SMPL → Render → Compare → Update. **Benefits**: Better accuracy, self-supervised improvement. --- ### PIFu / PIFuHD (2019-2020) **PIFu: Pixel-Aligned Implicit Function for High-Resolution Clothed Human Digitization** (Saito et al.) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.05172) **Key idea**: Implicit function for detailed clothed humans. **Architecture**: Pixel-aligned features → implicit SDF. **Benefits**: Handles clothing, high resolution. --- ### ARCH++ (2021) **ARCH++: Animation-Ready Clothed Human Reconstruction with Detailed Details** **Key innovation**: Detailed clothed human reconstruction, animation-ready. --- ## Core Concepts ### SMPL Fitting **Goal**: Estimate SMPL parameters from image. **Methods**: - Regression: Direct prediction with CNN - Optimization: Fit SMPL to 2D keypoints/silhouette - Hybrid: Regression + refinement --- ### Weak Supervision **Challenge**: Limited 3D ground truth data. **Solution**: Use 2D supervision (keypoints, silhouettes). **Losses**: - 2D keypoint reprojection - Silhouette matching - Adversarial shape prior --- ### Detailed Reconstruction **Beyond SMPL**: Add clothing, details, textures. **Methods**: - PIFu: Implicit function - Neural rendering: NeRF-style appearance - Displacement maps: Add details to SMPL --- ## Applications - Motion capture from video - Virtual try-on - Human animation - AR/VR avatars - Fitness tracking --- ## Related Modules - Module 9.1: Parametric Human Models (SMPL) - Module 9.2: Neural Humans (neural rendering) --- ## Additional Resources - **HMR**: Human mesh recovery implementations - **PIFu**: Detailed human reconstruction ---
