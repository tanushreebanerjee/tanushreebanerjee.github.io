# NeRF Appearance & Relighting **Module 5.3** | [← Back to Index](../index.md.html) | [Previous: NeRF Acceleration ←](05-nerf-acceleration.md.html) --- ## Overview NeRF appearance modeling: Decompose view-dependent appearance into shape, reflectance, and lighting. Enables relighting and material editing. --- ## Essential Papers ### NeRFactor (2021) **NeRFactor: Neural Factorization of Shape and Reflectance under an Unknown Illumination** (Bi et al.) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.04970) **Key idea**: Factorize NeRF into geometry, BRDF (material), and lighting. **Pipeline**: 1. Train NeRF to learn geometry and appearance 2. Extract surface geometry (marching cubes) 3. Optimize BRDF properties (albedo, roughness, normal) 4. Estimate lighting environment **Output**: Relightable scene with material properties. [Figure placeholder: NeRFactor factorization diagram] --- ### Ref-NeRF (2022) **Ref-NeRF: Structured View-Dependent Appearance for Neural Radiance Fields** (Verbin et al.) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.03907) **Key innovation**: Explicitly model view-dependent appearance using learned normal and reflection direction. **Appearance model**: Separates diffuse and specular components. **Benefits**: Better view-dependent modeling, enables relighting. --- ### Neural Reflectance Fields **Goal**: Decompose appearance into material properties. **Components**: Albedo, roughness, metallic, normal maps. **Applications**: Material editing, relighting, realistic rendering. --- ## Core Concepts ### Appearance Factorization **Geometry**: 3D shape (surface, volume). **Material**: BRDF properties (albedo, roughness, metallic). **Lighting**: Environment maps, light sources. **Combined**: Render realistic appearance under different lighting. --- ### View-Dependent Appearance **Challenge**: NeRF learns view-dependent color but doesn't separate causes. **Solution**: Explicitly model reflection direction, normal, lighting. **Benefits**: Better understanding, enables editing. --- ## Applications - Relighting scenes under new lighting - Material editing - Physically-based rendering - Virtual product visualization --- ## Related Modules - Module 5.1: NeRF Fundamentals (baseline) - Module 11.2: Inverse Rendering (overlapping concepts) --- ## Additional Resources - **NeRFactor Project Page**: Official implementation - **Inverse Rendering**: Related work on material estimation ---
